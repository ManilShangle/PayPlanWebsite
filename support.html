Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync with Google Calendar?
Open the extension, click “Sync to Google Calendar,” and grant permission. Events will be added to your calendar automatically.
How do I delete a payment from the calendar?
Click the payment on the calendar and select “Delete.” You'll be asked to confirm before it's removed from both the calendar and Google Calendar.
How can I disable BNPL detection?
Open the popup and click the "BNPL Detection" toggle in the top-left corner to turn detection off or on.
